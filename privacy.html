Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Nameck Studio (“Nameck Studio”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) provides tools and services for creating, managing, and publishing AI-generated entertainment videos and related media across supported platforms. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect information when you access our website, use our products, contact us, or connect third-party platform accounts such as TikTok.

We aim to collect only the information reasonably necessary to operate the service, support users, maintain security, comply with applicable law, and deliver the publishing features users request.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use Nameck Studio, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account and profile information, such as your name, email address, login credentials, account ID, company or studio name, and preferences.
  • Content and media, including videos, prompts, thumbnails, titles, captions, hashtags, descriptions, tags, and related metadata that you create, upload, edit, organize, or prepare for publishing.
  • Platform integration data, such as authorization tokens, account identifiers, creator profile information made available through authorized integrations, and platform permissions required to support a user-initiated publishing workflow.
  • Transaction and subscription information, such as plan type, billing status, invoices, payment references, and records needed to administer your account. Payment card details are typically processed by our payment providers and not stored directly by us unless expressly stated.
  • Technical, device, and usage information, including browser type, operating system, device identifiers, IP-derived information, error logs, timestamps, pages viewed, feature usage, referral URLs, and security signals.
  • Support and communication data, including information you send when contacting support, submitting feedback, or communicating with us by email or through the website.
  • Cookies and similar technologies, where used, to maintain sessions, remember preferences, measure performance, prevent abuse, and improve our services.

2. How We Use Information

We may use personal information and related data to:

  • provide, operate, maintain, and improve Nameck Studio and its creator tools;
  • enable users to create, manage, schedule, export, and publish AI-generated videos and related content through supported workflows;
  • authenticate users, secure accounts, detect unauthorized access, and prevent fraud or misuse;
  • provide customer support, answer questions, and respond to requests;
  • analyze usage trends, troubleshoot issues, monitor performance, and improve user experience;
  • enforce our Terms of Service, platform safety requirements, intellectual property protections, and internal compliance rules;
  • comply with legal obligations, respond to lawful requests, and protect the rights, property, safety, and integrity of Nameck Studio, our users, and third parties.

3. Platform Integrations and TikTok-Related Disclosures

If you choose to connect a third-party platform such as TikTok, we use the authorization and platform data made available to us only to support the workflow you requested. This may include identifying your connected account, enabling an upload or publishing flow, checking export status, or displaying basic creator information associated with the authorized integration.

Any posting, upload, or export feature is also subject to the technical limits, review conditions, community standards, and policy requirements of the relevant platform.
