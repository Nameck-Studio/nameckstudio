Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies specifically to FantasyGirls-Soft, a web application developed by Nameck Studio.

About FantasyGirls-Soft

FantasyGirls-Soft is a web application used to manage and publish AI-generated video content across supported platforms, including TikTok.

Information we process

FantasyGirls-Soft may process information necessary to operate the service, including:

  • account information provided by users,
  • video assets uploaded, created, or managed in FantasyGirls-Soft,
  • technical logs, security logs, and operational metadata,
  • authorization data related to connected third-party services.

TikTok connection

If a user connects a TikTok account to FantasyGirls-Soft, the application uses TikTok authorization only to support the user-requested authentication and content publishing workflow.

FantasyGirls-Soft does not publish content to a user's TikTok account without the user initiating the action and granting the required authorization.

Use of information

FantasyGirls-Soft uses information only as needed to:

  • operate the application,
  • manage publishing workflows,
  • maintain security and service reliability,
  • support connected platform integrations.

Data sharing

FantasyGirls-Soft does not sell personal data. Information may be processed by infrastructure or service providers only when necessary to operate the platform and its integrations.

Contact

For privacy-related questions about FantasyGirls-Soft, please contact: support@nameckstudio.com
