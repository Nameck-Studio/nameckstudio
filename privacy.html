Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 23, 2026

Nameck Studio provides a web platform for AI-assisted content generation, content management, and multi-platform publishing.

We collect and process only the information necessary to operate the service, including:

  • account information provided by users,
  • media assets created, uploaded, or managed within the platform,
  • technical logs and security-related data,
  • social platform authorization data when a user connects a third-party account.

If a user connects a TikTok account, Nameck Studio uses TikTok authorization only to enable the user-requested authentication and content export flow. We do not publish content to a user’s TikTok account without the user’s action and authorization.

We do not sell personal data.

Users may contact us for questions related to their account, their data, or deletion requests.

This page is provided as the public privacy information page for Nameck Studio and should be adapted with your final legal details, controller information, company details, and applicable jurisdiction before production use.
